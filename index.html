A Letter From Your Rollmate

To: My Rollmate, CSE 25 — BUET
From: Your Senior Rollmate, CSE

Hey, Waseka.

Welcome to the living hell. Onek koshto kore chance paiso BUET CSE te — and aro koshto kore tike o thakte hobe. BUET a tikar cheye ashole survive kora tai kotin.

Beshirbag student e vibinno college ar topper, vibinno valo valo jaiga theke ashche — sobar sathe tal miliye chola tai amar jonno main pera chilo. As ami akjon mid student chilam. 1-1 a sobar sobcheye beshi pora hoi programming a obbosto hothe. Beshirbag student programming age theke pare na ba shike ashe nai, ai karone ora ai semester a suffer kore. Tomake ami bolbo — jotho fast sombob programming shika. Of course class theke shikte parbe na — YouTube a vibinno resource ase, ami Code With Harry follow korsilam.

Coding korte obbosto hothe hobe. Online-offline thakbe, onek somoi kotin lagbe — na o parte paro — kinto hotash howa jabe na. Nijer admission time ta ke mone korba, ai kotin phase par kore ato mansoher sathe competition kore jehotu akon tikso, Allah tomi parbe bole tikaise. Tai try your best.

Ar attendance and CT te valo marks rakar try korio — na hole onek somoi CGPA tulte koshto hoie jai. Amar nijeri attendance 13 and CT 14 ta, tai amar result ar obosta 12 ta. Shekhane theke shikso.

Vibinno extra curricular activities a participate korar try korio — like hackathon. Ar hosse, akta path choose korte hobe: CP or Development.

If you love math and problem solving — go for CP. If you like to build — go for Development. Ami personally development choose korsi. Oneke CP o kore. Choice tomar.

In the end — Welcome again to BUET. Try your best to enjoy this time. Keep shining ✨ — and for any kind of problem, reach out to me anytime.

— A.K.M. Ashikul Islam
